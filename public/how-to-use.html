Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade to Premium?

Click on "Upgrade" in your profile settings or visit the Premium page to choose your plan.

Can I share my account?

For single-user accounts, sharing is not recommended. Consider family plans for household sharing.

What video formats are supported?

We support MP4, WebM, HLS, and DASH formats. Most modern video formats are compatible.
